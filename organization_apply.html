Liaison Position

Liaisons are an integral part of Raas Royalty. We are looking for fun, responsible students to be the guides of teams from other schools to show them Raas Royalty VIII and make the most of the weekend

Qualities of a good liaison are:

  • Be the biggest fan for the team you are representing

  • Keep the team on schedule during the competition with enthusiasm & excitement

  • Navigating the teams throughout campus and buildings

  • Being ready to handle questions and possible emergencies the teams have

If you are interested in becoming a part of our organization, please complete and submit the application by Sunday, October 16th by 12:00am. If you have any questions please email raasroyalty@gmail.com.
