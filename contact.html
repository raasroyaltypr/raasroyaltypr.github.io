General FAQ

When and where is the Raas Royalty Competition?

Raas Royalty will be held March 4th, 2017 at 7pm at the Indiana University Auditorium. Doors will open at around 6:30pm so please arrive early in order to be seated! The IU auditorium is located at 1211 E 7th St, Bloomington, IN 47405. Don't forget this is a FREE show open to all the public!

How can I get involved?

During the year we will open up applications for Freshman Representative positions and Liaison positions for Indiana University undergraduate students. These will be available in the Fall of 2016 so be on the lookout! Follow us on Facebook or email us at raasroyalty@gmail.com so we can put you on the list and be notified first.

Involvement with other IU organizations

Raas Royalty is always eager to partner with other student organizations or faculty members. If you would like to collaborate with us through smaller events through out the school year feel free to email us at raasroyalty@gmail.com.
