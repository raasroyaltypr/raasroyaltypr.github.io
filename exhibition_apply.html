Application Process

  • Each exhibition act must have completed the Team Registration form; follow the link above.

  • Each team must have photocopies of ALL participating members’ IDs (student ID AND driver’s license/state ID). Please make sure all photocopies are clear or else they will not be accepted. Also be sure that the photo IDs are all placed on one PDF page/document.

  • Each exhibition act must submit a video recording of a past performance. The video must be taken from this current year and contain at least 80% of those performing at Royalty on March 2nd (YouTube upload links will be accepted).

  • Please email all materials to raasroyaltylogistics@gmail.com by January,2019.

Applications will only be reviewed once all materials have been submitted. Please submit all of your registration materials at one time. Your application materials will be indexed with a time stamp and must be submitted before January 29th ,2019

*There is no guarantee for accommodations (travel and lodging), all request will be considered on a case by case basis.
