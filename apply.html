Judging

Our show would not be possible without talented individuals donating their time to judge our show and audition tapes. We accept applications from anyone with a background in dance. Please note, if you have competed during or after the 2012-2013 raas season, you are not eligible to judge for RRVIII, but we will gladly add you to our database for consideration next year. Please apply if you would like to considered as a judge for Raas Royalty VIII or be added to our database. For further information, please email Jill Patel at jillpate@umail.iu.edu.
